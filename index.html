FAQ

Common Questions

How much starting capital do I need?

You can open a paper trading simulator with zero risk. For live trading, a small account of $500 to $5,000 is the typical starting range. US traders under $25,000 are subject to PDT rules, which the free class covers in detail.

How long before I can trade live?

Most traders spend 30 to 90 days in the simulator logging paper trades before going live. The Warrior Method is a single pattern, so the learning curve is focused, but live psychology is a separate layer that only real money teaches.

Do I need to trade all day?

No. Ross trades from 9:30am to 11:00am Eastern. That is the window where the pattern lives. Afternoons are optional and, for most students, actively harmful to their P&L. The method is built for focused execution, not screen hours.

What markets do you trade?

Small-cap US equities that are gapping up on news in the first 30 minutes of the open. No options, no futures, no forex, no crypto. One asset class, one setup, repeated.

Who is this NOT for?

Traders looking for passive returns, signals to copy, or automated bots. The Warrior Method is an active trading discipline. If you will not keep a written plan, log trades, and respect stop losses, the method will not work regardless of the curriculum.
